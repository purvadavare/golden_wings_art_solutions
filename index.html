About Me

Hi, I am Aishwarya.

I love people and people love to feel beautiful, which is the reason I’ve spent last 7 years engulfed in doing makeup. I believe if you feel comfortable in your skin, then you can spend your energy loving others and enjoying life instead of worrying and being self-conscious. Is makeup the best way to gain self confidence? No way! But it can help a little, plus it’s FUN! So I hope I can help your life be a little bit happier, and perhaps more colorful. I’m available for various on-site make-up and hair artistry needs, including bridal and wedding parties, airbrushing, vintage styling, special occasions and photo shoots. I’m also more than happy to travel where needed. Looking forward to making some magic with you!
